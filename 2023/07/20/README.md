## S-Oil
- SK Securities predicts a low point in performance for S-Oil in 2Q23, with a projected revenue of 8.3 trillion won (-27.2% YoY, -8.3% QoQ) and operating profit of 371 billion won (-97.8% YoY, -92.8% QoQ).
- The petroleum chemical sector is expected to see increased profits due to improved spreads, while the lubricant sector is expected to maintain a solid OPM (28.0%) in 2Q23.
- However, the operating profit of the refining sector is expected to experience significant negative growth due to a decrease in shipments caused by regular maintenance in 2Q23, as well as a decline in refining margins and increased fixed cost burden and inventory-related losses due to a decrease in crude oil and down-stream prices. As a result, overall operating profit is expected to decline significantly.
