## NHN
- NHN recorded Q2 2023 operating revenue of KRW 551.4 billion (YoY +7.8%, QoQ +0.6%) and operating profit of KRW 20.9 billion (YoY +302.1%, QoQ +9.3%, OPM 3.8%), in line with consensus operating profit of KRW 203 billion.
- NHN has been aggressively investing in high-growth cloud business with data centers and R&amp;D, as well as reducing costs and downsizing in businesses with losses or slowed growth.
- Peacock and webtoons are expected to maintain their transaction volume and significantly improve their profitability, despite reducing marketing expenses.
- NHN's new AI data center in Gwangju will increase server capacity by more than 6 times after the completion of three new data centers, with operating costs estimated to be around KRW 2.5 billion per quarter. The high growth rate is expected to continue until 2028, considering the government's cloud conversion policy and progress.

## 수프로
국내 수목 유통 1위 기업으로, 전 세계적인 녹색 트렌드에 따른 수혜가 예상된다. 기후변화 대응을 위한 녹색 사업 확대와 환경, 도시녹화 사업 진출로 기업의 외형 확대도 예상된다.

- 어그테크 분야 신사업 발굴로 농산업 전문기업 도약
